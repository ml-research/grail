You are the player with a size of 8x24 points.
There are ladders in the game with a size of 8x35 points.
The whole game scene has a size of 320x420 points.
Now imagine a ladder being in the center of the scene.
Also imagine a 17x17 grid spread across the whole scene.
The player can be located in each grid cell.
Decide for each grid cell, if you think that the player (if it was located in that cell),
is [(predicate) left of | right of | on] the ladder such that the player is able to go [(action) right | left | up] to [(reason) reach the ladder | reach the ladder | climb the ladder up].
Output your decision as an array of arrays and assign each cell a value of
0.0 if you do not think so, 0.5 if you are unsure and 1.0 if you think so.
Think for a while and consider the game mechanics of the game.
Justify your answer by reflecting if your answer enables the player to achieve its goal
in that scene given how other objects in Kangaroo are typically placed.
Assume a usual game scene where the ladder happens to be naturally placed in the center.
End your answer with „RESULT:“ followed by the array.
Example:
